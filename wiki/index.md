# Evergreen Skills for Software Engineers

This wiki documents **"evergreen skills"** that should serve as a fair assessment of skilled software engineers. The focus is on **software development best practices, cross-framework principles, and portable skills**, as opposed to the detrimental fixation on language trends and framework-specific knowledge.

Programming languages evolve constantly, companies change their tech stacks, frameworks quickly become outdated. The skills that truly measure an engineer's value are those that **cannot be easily googled, take time to learn, are "evergreen," and have a huge impact on performance.**
